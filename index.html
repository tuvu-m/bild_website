/ How the app works

One job. A record that lasts forever.

Every BILD record is born on a job site. Capture on-site, the report writes itself, the property record updates, automatically.

01 · CAPTURE

The contractor captures, on-site

Voice notes and photos taken during the job, no forms, no laptop. Two taps before they pack up the van.

02 · STRUCTURE

BILD turns it into structure

What happens next is the part we don't show. The raw capture becomes verified, structured property data, mapped to the home.

03 · RECORD

The property record updates

The verified entry joins the home's permanent history, and feeds something the market doesn't have yet.
