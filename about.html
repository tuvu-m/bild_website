/ About us

We're building the trust layer for homes.

BILD is made by Luju Oy, a small team in Helsinki. We started with a simple frustration: the work that keeps homes standing leaves almost no trace. We're here to change that, one verified job at a time.

/ Why we exist

Homes are the biggest thing we own, and the least documented.

When a boiler is serviced or a roof is repaired, the knowledge walks out the door with the contractor. The next owner, the insurer, the bank, none of them can see what was actually done.

We think that's backwards. The people doing the work should be able to leave a verified record behind them effortlessly, and homeowners should own that record for the life of the home.

Start with the workflow. Earn the data. Build the trust layer the whole market has been missing.

/ What we believe

A few things we won't compromise on.

Verified or nothing

If it can't be traced back to real work by a real professional, it doesn't belong in the record.

The homeowner owns it

The record follows the home, not us. People should be able to take their history with them.

Earn the score

We won't ship a number until it deserves to be trusted. No shortcuts, no theatre.
